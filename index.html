Mission

Ocean Aware's purpose is to educate the public about issues that matter to the ocean and ocean life. We believe that we need to take care of our oceans because our oceans take care of us - even if we don't realize it.

Here at Ocean Aware, we offer resources for the public to educate themselves about the welfare of the ocean and ocean life either through self-education, or by attending one of our educational workshops, which can be accessed by signing up through our monthly newsletter.

Click the tabs above to explore educational articles and external resources. If you're interested in becoming a permanent member of our community, please sign up for our newsletter by filling out the fields on our newsletter page.

We hope you'll join us in protecting our oceans!
