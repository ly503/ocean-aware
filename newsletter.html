Ocean Aware Newsletter

Welcome to our Ocean-Aware monthly news letter.

We continue our fight to protect our oceans as they are the life support system of our planet.

The oceans are vital to human health as well, providing jobs, enjoyment and food to billions of people.

Our oceans are in more trouble than ever before.

Unsustainable industrial fishing practices are destroying habitats and endangering countless species.

Throwaway products like plastic water bottles and shopping bags often end up in the ocean and stay there for generations.

The time to end the damage to our oceans has arrived!

Ocean-Aware is partnering with Green Peace in a effort to fight for our oceans through marine reserves.

We are working towards establishing marine reserves in 20 percent of U.S. waters by 2020.

we want a better future for our oceans and we know you too !
